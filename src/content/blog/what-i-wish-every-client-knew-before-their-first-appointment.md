Hey beautiful souls! 💕

After years of welcoming new faces into my little beach bungalow studio, I've noticed there are a few things that would make your experience even more amazing if you knew them beforehand. Think of this as your gentle prep guide from your skincare-obsessed friend (that's me!).

## Come As You Are (But Maybe Skip the Makeup)

I know, I know – it feels weird to show up somewhere makeup-free. But honey, I'm going to take it all off anyway! Save yourself the trouble and come fresh-faced. If you absolutely must wear makeup for other reasons, no judgment here – I've got you covered with gentle cleansing that won't irritate your skin.

## Your Skin Has Secrets (And That's Okay!)

Don't feel embarrassed about breakouts, scarring, or that weird patch you've been worried about. I've literally seen it all, and nothing shocks me anymore. Your skin tells a story, and I'm here to listen without judgment. The more honest you are about your concerns and routine, the better I can customize your treatment.

## Timing is Everything

Here's a little insider secret: book your facial for evening if possible. Your skin might be a little pink afterward (totally normal!), and you'll want to baby it for the rest of the day. Also, avoid scheduling right before big events – give your skin at least a week to settle into its new glow.

## The "Purging" Phase is Real

If we're doing any kind of deep cleaning or chemical treatment, your skin might get a little worse before it gets better. This isn't me doing something wrong – it's actually a sign that we're bringing underlying issues to the surface so we can clear them out properly. Trust the process!

## Hydration Starts From Within

I can slather all the moisturizer in the world on your face, but if you're not drinking enough water, we're fighting an uphill battle. Start hydrating the day before your appointment, and keep it up afterward. Your skin will thank you!

## Sun Protection is Non-Negotiable

Especially here in beautiful North Myrtle Beach where the sun loves us year-round! After certain treatments like chemical peels or microneedling, your skin is more vulnerable to UV damage. I'll send you home with aftercare instructions, but the golden rule is always SPF 30 or higher.

## Questions Are Always Welcome

I'd rather you ask me something three times than leave confused. Whether it's about your treatment, aftercare, or why your skin does that weird thing when you eat dairy (spoiler alert: it might be connected!), I'm here for all your skincare curiosities.

## Your Home Routine Matters Most

Our hour together is amazing, but what you do at home for the other 23 hours of each day – that's where the real magic happens. I'll recommend products that actually work for your skin and budget, not just the most expensive ones on the shelf.

## It's Not Just Skincare, It's Soul Care

Finally, remember that this time is for YOU. Put your phone on silent, let yourself relax, and know that for the next hour or so, the only thing you need to worry about is how amazing you're going to feel walking out of here.
